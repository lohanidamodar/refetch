Refetch is a community-driven, open-source alternative to Hacker News — your daily drop of curated tech news, with signal over noise.

Discover the best of technology: AI, startups, open source, security, science, and programming, surfaced from 20+ trusted sources and ranked by a transparent, community-driven algorithm.

FEATURES
• Curated feed — browse Top, New, and Show stories, plus your own submissions
• Discussions — threaded comments with replies
• Voting — upvote and downvote stories and comments
• Submit — share a link or a "Show" post with the community
• Notifications — replies to you and an opt-in weekly digest of top stories
• Fast and clean, built for both mobile and desktop

Refetch is fully open source and privacy-focused, powered by Appwrite. Free from YC control — open, transparent, and community-run.

Learn more at https://refetch.io
